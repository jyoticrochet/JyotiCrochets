Who We Are

About Jyoti Crochets

Est. 2026Handcrafted Brand

Crafted with Purpose,
Designed with Heart

Jyoti Crochets is a premium handmade crochet brand established in 2026, born from a deep devotion to the timeless art of crochet and a commitment to creating pieces that transcend ordinary craftsmanship.

Every creation is meticulously handcrafted using carefully selected premium materials. We believe that handmade pieces carry a unique warmth, character, and soul that cannot be replicated by machines.

From elegant home accents to cozy wearables, each product embodies our dedication to quality, creativity, and the belief that beautiful things should be built to last.

Shipping Policy

Order Processing

All orders are processed within 2–5 business days. Each product is handcrafted with care, so production times may vary depending on the complexity of the item.

Handmade Production Time

Since every item is handmade, please allow 5–15 business days for crafting before shipping. We ensure every piece meets our quality standards before dispatch.

Estimated Delivery

Domestic delivery typically takes 5–10 business days after shipping. International shipping may take 15–30 business days depending on the destination.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on delivery location and order weight.

Tracking

A tracking number will be provided via email once your order ships.

Damaged Packages

If your package arrives damaged, contact us immediately at jyoticrochet5@gmail.com with photos.

Information

Return & Refund Policy

Eligibility

Returns accepted within 7 days of delivery for unused, undamaged items in original packaging.

Handmade Exceptions

Slight variations in color, size, and texture are natural characteristics of handmade items and are not grounds for return.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

Contact us within 48 hours at jyoticrochet5@gmail.com with photos for immediate resolution.

Refund Timeline

Approved refunds are processed within 7–14 business days to your original payment method.
